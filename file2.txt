Happy weekend. Rumour has it the cold is going to start biting, which makes it the perfect time to curl up with one of these reads…Not new but new-to-me is My Brilliant Friend by Elene Ferrente, the first in her Neopolitan novel cycle. A friend sent this to me when I was recuperating from my recent operation, and it was perfect escapism. I trod the hot, dusty streets of a poor neighbourhood in Naples; felt the sun on my face and the bond between childhood friends Elena and Lila (and their ambition to escape their beginnings, by whatever means).
